The biggest feast of the year. No hidden ingredients.

Feastival

FEASTival is San Francisco's beloved outdoor food festival featuring iconic live music performances, amazing food, and community-centric events that highlight the food, not the chemicals. Celebrating food, community, and food transparency for over 50 years every year right here in San Francisco.

Food

Of course, there is no feast without food, so there is no shortage of vendors from all over the Bay Area to accomodate to your tastes. Enjoy over 30 selections to choose from to elevate your dietary palatte.

Music

Enjoy tasty bites while listening to the Bay Area's finest independent artists that range from genres of classical, soft rock, and smooth jazz.

Vendors

Browse through local and handmade household and artful wares and pieces made by Bay Area artists. Choose between award winning dinnerware, handmade ceramic vases, and signed cookbooks by local chefs.

Community Events

Take part in our exciting annual pie-eating contest, a thrifted fashion show, and volunteer to serve your community at the events.
